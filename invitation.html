Dear Producer,

On behalf of the HIRO 2026 Organising Committee, I am pleased to invite you to the 1st International Workshop on Shinosawa Hiro (HIRO 2026), held at Ciyuan Chahui, Jiangning District, Nanjing, on December 20, 2026.

HIRO 2026 is a unique hybrid of an academic workshop and a birthday celebration, bringing together scholars, artists, enthusiasts, and devotees to explore the multifaceted brilliance of Shinosawa Hiro through academic exchange, artistic exhibition, and communal festivity.

The programme includes oral presentations, poster exhibitions, business card exchange, video screenings, a reception, and a birthday celebration. We welcome research papers, abstracts, fan works (illustrations, photography, fan fiction, and more), and other creative formats, with the option to present orally, by poster, or for proceedings only.

Given your passion for Shinosawa Hiro, your participation would greatly enrich the workshop. We would be delighted to have you join us on this special occasion.
