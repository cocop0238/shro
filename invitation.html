Dear Producer,

I am writing on behalf of the HIRO 2026 Organising Committee to cordially invite you to attend the 1st International Workshop on Shinosawa Hiro (HIRO 2026), which will be held at Ciyuan Chahui, Jiangning District, Nanjing, Jiangsu Province, China, on December 20, 2026.

HIRO 2026 is a unique hybrid of an academic workshop and a birthday celebration. The workshop aims to serve as a premier platform for exploring the multifaceted brilliance of Shinosawa Hiro through academic exchange, artistic exhibition, and communal festivity. Our goal is to bring together scholars, artists, enthusiasts, and devotees from all disciplines to share their works inspired by or dedicated to Shinosawa Hiro.

The programme will include oral presentations, poster and art displays, a reception, and a birthday celebration. We welcome contributions across a wide range of topics under the unifying umbrella of Shinosawa Hiro, including scholarly research, fan fiction, fan art, and other creative formats.

Given your passion for Shinosawa Hiro, we believe that your participation would greatly enrich the discussions and festivities of the workshop. We would be delighted if you could join us on this special occasion.

For registration and submission details, please visit our conference website: https://cocop0238.github.io/shro/

I look forward to celebrating with you at HIRO 2026.
